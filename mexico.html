About Mexico

Mexico, located in North America, is a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the ancient Mayan ruins to the vibrant streets of Mexico City, the country offers a diverse array of experiences. Known for its delicious cuisine, colorful festivals, and warm hospitality, Mexico continues to be a popular destination for travelers seeking a blend of tradition and modernity.
