About USA

The United States of America, commonly known as the USA, is a vast country located in North America. It is comprised of 50 states, each with its unique culture and attractions. Renowned for its diverse landscapes, the USA boasts iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Grand Canyon, and the White House. With a rich history and a global influence in politics, economics, and culture, the USA stands as a symbol of freedom and opportunity.
