About China

China, the world's most populous country, is situated in East Asia with a history spanning thousands of years. Known for its ancient landmarks such as the Great Wall and the Forbidden City, China seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. Boasting a diverse landscape, from the bustling metropolises like Beijing and Shanghai to the picturesque landscapes of Guilin, China captivates with its cultural richness. As a global economic powerhouse, China plays a pivotal role in shaping international affairs and technological advancements.
