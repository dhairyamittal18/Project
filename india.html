About India

India, known for its rich history and diverse culture, is a country in South Asia. Home to the Taj Mahal, vibrant festivals, and a variety of landscapes from the Himalayas to tropical beaches, India is a melting pot of traditions and modernity. As one of the world's fastest-growing economies, India continues to make significant contributions to technology, science, and the arts.
